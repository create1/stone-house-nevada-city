Rehearsal Dinners

The night before is when the energy starts building. Host your rehearsal dinner at Stone House and give your closest people a preview of the magic to come.

The Penthouse

Your Private Upstairs Dining Room

The Penthouse is the natural home for rehearsal dinners — a rectangular room on the upper level (26'5" x 34'1") with two full bar stations, a head table for the couple and parents, and enough room for 55–80 guests. It's self-contained, intimate, and distinctly Stone House.

What's Included

Every rehearsal dinner at Stone House comes with the same level of coordination and attention your wedding day receives.

  • Private use of The Penthouse
  • 3-hour venue access
  • On-site coordinator
  • Head table for couple + family
  • Two bar stations (full service)
  • Farm-to-table dinner (choice of tier)
  • Table settings and linens
  • Candles and ambient lighting
  • Sound system for toasts and music
  • Dedicated wait staff

Bundle and save. When you book your rehearsal dinner and wedding together at Stone House, your coordinator manages both events seamlessly. One venue, one team, one less thing to worry about the week of your wedding.
Other Options

Beyond The Penthouse

For smaller rehearsal dinners (under 30 guests), The Parlor offers a more casual cocktail-style evening with the L-shaped bar, lounge seating, and round cocktail tables. For larger groups or summer events, The Courtyard provides an outdoor option that pairs beautifully with a passed-appetizer format.

Your coordinator will help you choose the right space based on your guest count, vibe, and the logistics of your wedding day setup happening simultaneously.
