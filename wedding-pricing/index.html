Within a beautifully restored 1857 historic building, Stone House blends timeless charm with a modern event venue and elevated hospitality. Our catering is crafted with the highest quality ingredients and always seed oil free.

Our Spaces

Six distinct spaces with four rooms, all under one roof.

The Showroom

Featuring a state-of-the-art sound and lighting system and wooden dance floor, there is no better place in the Sierra Foothills to dance the night away.

Up to 300 cocktails · 50 seated dinner

The Parlour

A mesmerizing bar and exquisite cocktails await you in a casual and riveting space downstairs. Cocktail service or connect to the Showroom for a larger event.

Up to 100 · 200 combined w/ Showroom

The Lounge

The finest craft cocktails, plush leather seating, and gorgeous wood tables. This majestic room will leave your guests in awe.

Up to 50 · 150 combined w/ Great Hall

The Great Hall

Stone walls, timber beams and elegant lighting set the backdrop for your group's formal dining experience at rustic wooden tables.

Up to 100 seated guests

The Courtyard

Perfect for an outdoor ceremony and al fresco dining. Framed by romantic stone walls, an intimate wine cavern, and a picturesque brick stairway against the backdrop of Nevada City's nature.

130 ceremony · 100 seated dinner

The Cavern

Built in the late 1800s as a brewery, make it your wedding hideaway, invite guests for wine and whiskey tastings, or host a small dinner.

Up to 30 guests

The Penthouse Suite

Our 3-bedroom penthouse suite is the perfect place to accommodate your daytime relaxation and overnight stay. Equipped with a steam shower, copper tub, and kitchen for your every need.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is required to secure your date?

We require a 50% deposit in order to secure your date.

If it's an outdoor ceremony, what's the plan B if it rains?

If it rains, we will move the ceremony to the Showroom as the backup indoor location.

Are you pet-friendly?

Yes, we are pet-friendly.

How close are you to hotels and local entertainment?

We are one block from The National Exchange Hotel.

What is the timing of a typical wedding day?

A typical wedding day runs from 11:00 AM to 11:00 PM.

Do I need to hire a wedding planner?

Yes, a wedding planner is required. You may also hire one of our wedding coordinators.
