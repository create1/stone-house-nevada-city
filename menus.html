Bar Program

Craft Cocktails & Local Wines

Every Stone House bar features a curated selection of local wines from Sierra Foothills and Nevada County wineries, craft beers from regional breweries, and a signature cocktail menu designed for your event. Non-alcoholic options include house-made sodas, mocktails, and specialty coffee and tea service.

Dietary flexibility built in. Every menu tier can accommodate gluten-free, dairy-free, vegan, vegetarian, and allergy-specific needs. Let us know during your tasting and we'll build alternatives that are just as good as the main menu — never an afterthought.
