Getting Ready

Private prep spaces where the day begins. Natural light, original stone walls, and everything your team needs — without leaving the venue.

The Spaces

Dedicated Suites for Both Parties

Stone House provides separate getting-ready spaces so both wedding parties can prepare in private, on-site, with no need to shuttle between a hotel and the venue. Your photographer will love the light, and you'll love having everything in one place.

The Bridal Suite

A private room with soft natural light, original 1857 stone walls, and the kind of atmosphere that makes getting-ready photos look effortless. Comfortably fits a wedding party of 6–8 plus hair and makeup artists.

  • Full-length mirrors
  • Natural daylight + warm lighting
  • Multiple power outlets for styling tools
  • Seating for wedding party
  • Garment rack and steamer access
  • Private restroom
  • Beverage service available
  • Climate controlled

The Groom's Room

A separate space for the other party to get ready with equal privacy and comfort. Original stone and wood finishes create a handsome backdrop for portraits and candid moments.

  • Full-length mirror
  • Comfortable seating
  • Power outlets
  • Garment hanging space
  • Beverage service available
  • Climate controlled
For Photographers

Designed for Beautiful Getting-Ready Photos

The getting-ready spaces at Stone House are a photographer's dream. The combination of natural light filtering through historic windows and the texture of original stone walls means your photos have character and warmth without any staging.

Photographer tip: The bridal suite gets the best natural light from mid-morning through early afternoon. For winter weddings, request additional lighting from our team — we have warm LED options that complement the stone beautifully.

Suggested Getting-Ready Timeline

4–5 hrsBefore ceremony — Hair & makeup team arrives, begins setup
3.5 hrsBefore ceremony — Wedding party arrives, getting-ready begins
2 hrsBefore ceremony — Photographer arrives for candid getting-ready shots
1.5 hrsBefore ceremony — Detail shots (dress, rings, shoes, invitations)
1 hrBefore ceremony — Final touches, first look or pre-ceremony portraits
30 minBefore ceremony — Wedding party in position, guests seated
What to Bring

Packing for the Day

Stone House provides the space, seating, mirrors, and power. Here's what to bring along: your attire on hangers, personal toiletries, any special lighting your makeup artist prefers, a Bluetooth speaker for your getting-ready playlist, snacks and drinks for the morning (or let us handle it with a getting-ready beverage package), and your photographer's shot list so nothing gets missed.

Leave the stress at the door. Your Stone House coordinator will have the suites prepped, climate controlled, and ready when you arrive. If you need anything during prep — extra hangers, a steamer, an emergency sewing kit — just ask. We've seen it all and we've got it covered.
