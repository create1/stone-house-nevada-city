Where to Stay for Your Stone House Wedding

Nevada City is a walkable historic town with charming lodging at every price point — most within a few blocks of the venue. No shuttles needed.

Luxury & Boutique

Premier Properties

For the couple and VIP guests who want the full experience — historic elegance and top-tier service.

Luxury

The National Exchange Hotel

0.2 miles — same street, walkable
From $301/night

Fully restored Victorian landmark with 38 rooms, fine dining, and spa services. The premier lodging closest to Stone House — your guests can walk back after the reception.

Room blocks available for weddings · Suites & King Suites · Full-service event support
Luxury

The Holbrooke Hotel

6 miles — Grass Valley
$150–$250/night

Gold Rush–era boutique hotel (1862) with 28 uniquely appointed rooms, a historic saloon, and Victorian charm. Walkable to Grass Valley's downtown.

Room blocks available · Fireplaces & clawfoot tubs · On-site restaurant & bar
Charming & Mid-Range

B&Bs & Inns

Nevada City has more Victorian B&Bs per block than almost anywhere in California. Each one is unique.

Mid-Range

Piety Hill Cottages

Same street as Stone House
From $139/night

Private cottages with full hot breakfast delivered to your room. Quiet, tucked-away retreat that's literally next door to the venue. Ideal for the bridal party.

1–3 room cottages · Private baths · Breakfast included
Mid-Range

Red Castle Inn

0.5 miles — walkable
$160–$230/night

Gothic Revival B&B from 1860, perched on a wooded hillside with sweeping views of Nevada City. Often considered the town's premier bed and breakfast.

Historic landmark · Garden terraces · Full breakfast
Mid-Range

Emma Nevada House

0.4 miles — walkable
$150–$220/night

Elegant Victorian B&B built in 1856 with beautiful gardens and creek access. Named after Nevada City's famed opera singer.

Gardens & stream · Walking distance · Breakfast included
Mid-Range

Broad Street Inn

0.3 miles — walkable
$140–$215/night

Six-room historic B&B (1870) in a peaceful garden setting, plus a private Carriage House apartment. Perfect for a small bridal party buyout.

6 rooms + carriage house · Garden setting · Downtown location
Mid-Range

Deer Creek Inn

0.3 miles — walkable
$150–$200/night

Queen Anne Victorian B&B near Deer Creek. Comfortable rooms with modern amenities in a historic setting.

Walking distance to venue · Near Deer Creek trail · Breakfast included
Mid-Range

Flume's End B&B

0.4 miles — walkable
$150–$200/night

Victorian retreat (1860) with private decks, creek views, and a separate cottage. Five rooms plus a standalone cottage for extra privacy.

Private decks · Creek & mountain views · Cottage option
Mid-Range

The Parsonage 1865

0.3 miles — walkable
$130–$175/night

Mid-1800s bed and breakfast with six rooms and a private cottage with kitchenette. Renovated with private bathrooms throughout.

6 rooms + cottage · Kitchenette available · Near Tahoe National Forest trails
Mid-Range

Gold Miners Inn

6 miles — Grass Valley
$126–$235/night

Full-service 81-room hotel with hot breakfast, evening cocktails, pool, and event spaces. The best option for guests wanting standard hotel amenities.

Room blocks available · 81 rooms · Pool · Complimentary breakfast & cocktails
Value & Budget-Friendly

Affordable Options

Comfortable lodging that keeps the budget for what matters — the celebration.

Budget

Northern Queen Inn

0.7 miles
From $71/night

86 rooms including secluded cabins and chalets with full kitchens. Year-round pool and spa. Best value for families needing space.

Room blocks available · Cabins & chalets · Pool & spa · Kitchens available
Budget

Outside Inn

0.5 miles — walkable
Under $130/night

Renovated 1940s motor court — fresh, affordable, and two blocks from downtown. Non-smoking, fun retro vibe.

Renovated retro charm · Near downtown · Budget-friendly
Budget

US Hotel B&B

0.2 miles — walkable
Budget to mid-range

California State Historical Landmark (1853) with featherbeds, clawfoot tubs, and a three-course breakfast. Seven rooms of Gold Rush character.

State landmark · Featherbeds · Three-course breakfast
Budget

Nevada City Inn

0.8 miles
~$130/night

Straightforward, comfortable inn. A solid fallback for guests who want simple, affordable lodging close to town.

Basic amenities · Close to town · No-fuss lodging
Group & Family Options

Vacation Rentals

For bridal parties, families, and groups who want to stay together.

Airbnb & VRBO

$200–$600+/night for full-house rentals

Nevada City has dozens of Victorian homes available for full-house rental — ideal for bridal parties wanting a private getaway. Search for Nevada City, Grass Valley, and Penn Valley for the widest selection.

Full homes · Private · Kitchens · Perfect for bridal party weekends

Creekside Cottages

6 miles — Grass Valley

Six unique cottages in historic downtown Grass Valley. Ideal for wedding parties and family reunions who want individual units in one location.

6 cottages · Group-friendly · Downtown Grass Valley
Room Block Tip: The National Exchange Hotel, Gold Miners Inn, and Northern Queen Inn all accommodate room blocks for weddings. Contact them early — Nevada City is a popular destination town, and weekends book up months in advance. Mention that you're hosting your wedding at Stone House.
Distance Note: Everything in Nevada City is within a mile of Stone House at 107 Sacramento Street. Most guests won't need a car during their stay. Grass Valley is 6 miles south on Highway 49 with additional options. For the adventurous: Lake Tahoe is about an hour east.
