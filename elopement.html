Why Stone House

An Elopement Worth the Name

Stone House isn't a blank-slate ballroom. It's 170 years of history carved into every stone wall, every arch, every candlelit alcove. When you elope here, the venue does the heavy lifting — no elaborate decor required. The space is the statement.

Your coordinator handles everything from vendor coordination to timeline management, so your only job on the day is to show up and say yes. And because our kitchen operates the same way for 2 guests as it does for 200, your dinner will be the same farm-to-table, seed-oil-free experience that makes Stone House dining unforgettable.
