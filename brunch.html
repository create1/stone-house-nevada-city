Day-After Brunch

The celebration doesn't have to end at last call. Gather your people the morning after for one more meal together before everyone heads home.

At Stone House

On-Site Brunch

For couples who want to keep the magic going, Stone House offers a next-morning brunch option right at the venue. Your guests wake up, walk over, and find the same space that hosted last night's celebration now set for a relaxed, sunlit morning together.

Stone House Morning After

Available 10:00 AM – 1:00 PM · Up to 80 guests

A casual, buffet-style brunch in The Penthouse or The Courtyard (weather permitting). The same seed-oil-free kitchen that prepared your wedding dinner turns out pastries, frittatas, fresh fruit, and house-made granola. Mimosa and Bloody Mary stations included.

This option is especially popular for destination weddings where most guests are staying nearby — it gives everyone one final gathering before checkout and departure.

Add it to your package. On-site brunch is available as an add-on when you book your wedding at Stone House. Your coordinator will handle all the logistics so you can just show up, coffee in hand.
Around Town

Nevada City Brunch Spots

Nevada City has a strong breakfast and brunch scene. These spots are walking distance or a short drive from Stone House and most can accommodate groups with advance notice.

Lika's Quarter Cafe

0.2 miles from Stone House

Cajun-influenced breakfast at the historic 401 Commercial St location. A neighborhood favorite with a patio perfect for groups. Reservations recommended for parties of 6+.

The Place on South Pine

0.3 miles from Stone House

Hearty, healthy, fresh and local fare at 110 South Pine St. A welcoming spot with indoor seating and a garden patio.

Nevada City Classic Cafe

0.4 miles from Stone House

French-American cafe on Broad Street, open since the 1950s. Hearty breakfasts with generous portions. Good for large groups — they have a back room that fits 15–20.

Friar Tuck's

0.3 miles from Stone House

Weekend brunch from 11 AM to 2 PM with non-GMO, locally sourced menu items. A Nevada City institution with a full bar and relaxed atmosphere.

Three Forks Bakery & Brewing Co.

0.5 miles from Stone House

Artisan bakery with house-brewed beer. Fresh pastries, breakfast sandwiches, and coffee. Casual atmosphere perfect for a low-key morning after.

Grass Valley Options

~15 min drive

For groups staying in Grass Valley, Cirino's and Tofanelli's offer excellent brunch options with private dining rooms that can host wedding groups of 20–40.

Let your coordinator help. If you'd like to organize a group brunch at a local restaurant, your Stone House coordinator can help with reservations, transportation logistics, and even a custom menu if the restaurant offers it. Just let us know during planning.
